F.A.Q.

Correct ≠ Complex.

What’s the difference between the services?

→ One-Page Lander™ is a custom one-page site with 1 light revision.
→ Three-Page Custom™ is a custom three-page site with 2 light revisions.
→ 30-Day BNDR Unlimited™ is 30 calendar days of direct work for one business.

What can be requested?

30-Day Unlimited includes unlimited requests including: Websites, Refinements on Existing Content, AI agents, Integrations, Branding, Data Analysis, Bottleneck Breakouts, Marketing Materials, Services, Graphic design, Dashboards, Web applications, and more.

How fast is delivery?

When a request goes active, it gets a stated delivery window. Larger requests ship in checkpoints until complete.

How do requests work?

For 30-Day BNDR Unlimited™, unlimited requests can be queued. One request is active at a time, if it’s something smaller, at times I may have the room to double-up, but not always.

How do revisions work?

One-Page Lander™ includes 1 light revision. Three-Page Custom™ includes 2 light revisions. 30-Day BNDR Unlimited™ includes unlimited revisions during the active month.

What is a light revision?

One consolidated round submitted within 7 calendar days of delivery.

How does onboarding work?

A short alignment exchange by email: goals, priority, links/assets, and what “done” means to you for any given request. Then I get to work, and send you the results, and we go from there.

How fast do you start?

All payments are taken upfront. Work begins the same day payment clears.

How do we communicate?

Call, Email, Text, whatever is easiest for you. A shared board can be added. Something like private ChatGPT space can be included.

Do you use AI?

Yes. AI is part of my workflow across web services, with a heavy human hand & eye in the loop.

Do you build in Webflow?

No. You're getting the real deal in coded form. Ready to launch, or launched (Hosted) for you.

How many people from my team can be involved?

Unlimited participants. Only those who you assign as 'designated approvers' sign off on final decisions.

What about images and visual assets?

Brand-matched visuals can be created and tailored to your style. Third-party assets can be licensed for your use, with the license details passed through.

How does hosting work?

Hosting is included with 30-Day BNDR Unlimited™ while active, upon request. Hosting is available separately for One-Page Lander™ and Three-Page Custom™ at $60/mo per site.

What happens outside the selected package?

Additional work beyond the selected package is billed at $75/hr.

What’s the refund policy?

30-Day BNDR Unlimited™ is month-to-month. Cancel anytime. It includes a 1-week 75% money-back guarantee. After the first week, the month remains active through day 30 and is non-refundable. All other work is non-refundable. While this is true, it's important to remember that client satisfaction is a primary driving force behind my work.
